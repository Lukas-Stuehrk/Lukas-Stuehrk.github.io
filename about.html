About Lukas

Professional

I've been working in software engineering for more than a decade by now. I started my career as a frontend developer working on e-commerce websites and web apps, but specialized in mobile apps early in my career. Nowadays, I focus on building native apps for Apple's platforms such as iOS, iPadOS, and macOS.

Currently, I'm working in the role of an individual contributor. However, I also managed both software engineering teams as a tech lead and engineering manager, as well as other engineering managers as a head of engineering. My teams have a proven track record in shipping and maintaining high-quality software over long periods of time.

I'm a big proponent of remote work and have worked in nearly every possible team-setup, such as onsite teams, nearshored teams in the same timezone, and fully distributed teams with different time zones.

My experience spans from working in tiny startups that are still searching for their market fit to publicly traded companies with complex organization structures.

During my career, I worked in the domains of digital publishing, banking and fintech, e-commerce, and video conferencing.
